History

Here's a look at the marvelous history of Lancia 

  • 1953 - The birth of scuderia Lancia

    the manufacturer participated in several races like the 24th hours of Le Mans or the famous Mille-miglia

  • 1963->1972 - the Fulvia domination

    Lancia won several manufacturers title with the hatchback "fulvia"

  • 1986 - The tragic accident of Toivonnen

    In 1986, Lancia pilot Henry Toivonnen crashed during the Tour de Corse, causing its death. This accident among many others signed the death of group B racing and of the lancia delta S4

  • 1987->1992 - Lancia Delta Integrale absolute domination

    Following the accident, the Lancia Delta has been adapted to run into group A category, and managed to win 3 titles consecutively with the maximum amount of point, which has never been seen before
